What is the Nanjing Massacre?

The Nanjing Massacre took place in December, 1937, in the city of Nanjing, then the capital of China.

Why Do We Care?

It is indescribably brutal, and it's a great humiliation to humanity.

In Memory of Our People

Memorial events take place in China every year. In the city of Nanjing, alarms are run every year to remind people of the brutal slaughter.
